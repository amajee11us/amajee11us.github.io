IoT based reconfiguration of microgrids through an automated central protection centre

Paper Details

  • Authors: Anay Majee, OV Gnana Swathika
  • Published in : 2017 International Conference on Power and Embedded Drive Control
  • Domain: Intelligent Microgrid Reconfiguration
  • Paper link: ieeexplore.ieee.org/abstract/document/8081066
    In this paper we aim to develop an automated Central Processing Centre (CPC) based on the concept of Internet of Things (IoT) which would constantly monitor the grid for the detection of faults and rectify them. The rectification of fault is either done by isolating the faulted bus or finding the shortest path from the faulted bus to the main grid.
